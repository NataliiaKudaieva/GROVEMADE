We've been operating in our hometown of Portland, Oregon since 2009. We reflected on who we are and what makes us Grovemade—here is our story.

Go back

Grovemade is headquartered in a 8400 ft2 former casket factory originally built in 1922. We are lucky to be in a quiet residential neighborhood across the street from one of the premier parks in Portland. A typical day starts with a cool breeze rolling in through the wide open garage doors as people trickle in with a cheery “Good morning”, sleeves rolled up ready to go. We do design, marketing, supply chain management, and limited high skill manufacturing all under one roof—there are many different kinds of work to do and so little time to do it.
